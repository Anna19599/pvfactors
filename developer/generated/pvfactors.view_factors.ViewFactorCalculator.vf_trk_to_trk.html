pvfactors.view_factors.ViewFactorCalculator.vf_trk_to_trk¶

static ViewFactorCalculator.vf_trk_to_trk(line_1, line_2, *args)[source]¶

Use parallel plane formula to calculate view factor between PV rows. This assumes that pv row #2 is just a translation along the x-axis of pv row #1; meaning that only their x-values are different and they have the same elevation or rotation angle.

Parameters:
  • line_1 (shapely.LineString) – Line for surface 1: pv row or ground
  • line_2 (shapely.LineString) – Line for surface 2: pv row or ground
  • *args (tuple) –
Returns:

calculated view factor vf
Return type:

float
