timeseries.TsDualSegment¶

class timeseries.TsDualSegment(coords, illum_ts_surface, shaded_ts_surface, index=None, n_vector=None)[source]¶

A TsDualSegment is a timeseries segment that can only have 1 shaded surface and 1 illuminated surface. This allows indexing of the object.

__init__(coords, illum_ts_surface, shaded_ts_surface, index=None, n_vector=None)[source]¶

Initialize timeseries dual segment using segment coordinates and timeseries illuminated and shaded surfaces.

Parameters

  • coords (TsLineCoords) – Timeseries coordinates of full segment

  • illum_ts_surface (TsSurface) – Timeseries surface for illuminated part of dual segment

  • shaded_ts_surface (TsSurface) – Timeseries surface for shaded part of dual segment

  • index (int, optional) – Index of segment (Default = None)

  • n_vector (np.ndarray, optional) – Timeseries normal vectors of the side (Default = None)
